Za'atar & Zza's

A Place to Gather, A View to Remember

At Za'atar & Zza's, we believe food tastes best when it brings people together. That's why we've created a space that feels relaxed, friendly, and full of Cape Town charm. Whether you're enjoying a family meal, catching up with friends, or planning a cosy date night, you'll feel right at home here. Choose a table indoors for a comfortable, inviting atmosphere, or sit outside and soak up uninterrupted views of Lion's Head and Table Mountain – the perfect backdrop to any meal.

Our menu is designed with something for everyone. From wood-fired pizzas and smoky kebabs to creamy pastas, fresh salads, and indulgent desserts like Kunafe, Tiramisu, and authentic Italian gelato, every dish is made with care and flavour. Families will love our dedicated kids' menu, while adults can enjoy colourful mocktails, smoothies, milkshakes, or a perfectly brewed coffee. Proudly Halaal-certified, Za'atar & Zza's is all about inclusive dining – a place where quality, variety, and flavour come together.
